Antonio Bello

I define myself as a code artist and polyglot developer. During my professional experience, I’ve worked with over a dozen languages (C/C++, java, Objective C, C#, javascript, python just to name a few)..

I make of learning one of the most important aspects of my work.

I dedicate myself on building great software, writing beautiful code, and bringing life to ideas.

If you need help on any project, regardless of its type and the involved languages and technologies, and you think I can be a good fit, just write me. I am available for short and long term projects.

My History

My first computer was a Commodore Vic20. Simply an amazing personal computer, 3583 bytes of RAM (!!). With it I learnt how to make programs, how to deal with limited resources (and believe me, they were really limited). It was long long ago, so long that a lot of people would be telling me things like “what’s a computer?” or “computers have no future, don’t waste time on them”.
